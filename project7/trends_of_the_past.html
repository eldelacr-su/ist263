So When Did Sneakers Become So Culturally Important?

IN THE 1980S -

WITH THE GROWTH OF STREETWEAR IN BASKETBALL AND SKATEBOARDING, HIP-HOP EMERGENCE AND MULTIPLE CULTURE SHIFTS, THE SNEAKER CULTURE WAS BORN. SNEAKERS HAVE BEEN AN IMPORTANT PART TO FASHION EVER SINCE AND STREETWEAR GREW TO BECOME A NORM. THE EVOLUTION OF THE SNEAKER CULTURE HAS BEEN BEAUTIFUL AND WE WANT TO SHOWCASE THE HISTORY…

TOP TRENDS IN THE 2010S

KD, LEBRON, KOBE ALL LED THE DECADE WITH SILLOUTES WHO RAN THE STREETS. JORDANS PEEKED AND HAD A DROPOFF AND THE YEEZY SHIFT TOOK OVER. 2010’S WAS A TIME FOR THE SNEAKER WORLD WHERE YOU HAD TO KEEP UP.

TOP TRENDS IN THE 2000S

JORDANS WERE EVERYWHERE. EVERY SINGLE KID HAD, WANTED … NEEDED JORDANS. THE RETROS THE NEW ONES WHICHEVER THEY WERE ALL SELLING. ADIDAS WAS ON THE COME UP WITH AI AND SHAQ. NIKE SB WAS KILLING IT IN THE SKATEBOARD INDUSTRY BUT HAD A SHARP FALL OFF NOT TOO SHORT AFTER…

TOP TRENDS IN THE 1990S

CONVERSE, ADIDAS, NIKE ALL FIGHTING TO STAMP THEIR NAMES AND BECOME JUGGERNAUGHTS. A ROUGH TIME IN SNEAKER CULTURE BESIDES FOR JORDAN. HIS INFLUENCE WAS AMAZING. TENNIS SHOES WERE STILL LEADERS.

TOP TRENDS IN THE 1980S

THE BEGINNING OF THE STREETWEAR CULTURE. THE START OF ATHLETIC SHOES BEING FUN TO WEAR AND COOL TO HAVE. NIKE AND PHIL KNIGHT DESIGNED BEAUTIFUL SHOES, A LOT OF OTHER COMPETITORS BROUGHT SHOES TO SPOTLIGHT AS WELL.
